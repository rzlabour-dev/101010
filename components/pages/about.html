About PDF Tools

Our Mission

PDF Tools is a collection of free, client-side utilities for working with PDF files. All processing happens directly in your browser, ensuring your documents remain private and secure.

Privacy First

Your files never leave your computer. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Fast & Efficient

No uploads or downloads to external servers means faster processing times.

Open Source

Built with open source libraries like pdf.js and JSZip. Completely free to use.
