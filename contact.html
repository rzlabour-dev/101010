Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDF Tools Pro really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no watermarks on your converted files.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer or get uploaded to our servers.

What file formats are supported?

For PDF to Images: PDF files only. For Images to PDF: JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and other common image formats.

Can I use PDF Tools Pro offline?

Yes! You can download the source code from GitHub and run it locally on your computer without an internet connection.
