Our Mission

PDF Tools Pro was created with a simple mission: to provide free, high-quality PDF conversion tools that respect your privacy. We believe that everyone should have access to essential PDF tools without compromising their data security.

Why We're Different

100% Private

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your computer or get uploaded to our servers.

Completely Free

No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no watermarks. All our tools are free to use now and forever.

Open Source

Our code is open for inspection and contribution. Transparency builds trust in our tools.

How It Works

PDF Tools Pro uses modern web technologies to process files entirely in your browser:

  • PDF.js: For reading and rendering PDF files
  • jsPDF: For creating PDF documents from images
  • JSZip: For creating ZIP archives
  • Canvas API: For image processing and conversion
