Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Learn how we protect your data.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process any of your files on our servers.

How We Protect Your Privacy

PDF Tools Pro is designed with privacy as the core principle:

Local Processing

All file conversions happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your computer.

No Server Uploads

We don't have a backend server to upload files to. The entire application runs client-side.

Minimal Cookies

We use only essential cookies for site functionality. No tracking cookies are used.

Anonymous Analytics

If we use analytics, they're anonymized and don't collect personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

  • Files: We never see, store, or have access to your uploaded files
  • Personal Information: We don't require accounts or personal details
  • Usage Data: We don't track which files you convert or when
  • IP Addresses: When hosted on static platforms, they may log IPs, but we don't

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CDN Services: For loading libraries (pdf.js, JSZip, etc.)
  • Font Awesome: For icons
  • Hosting Platforms: GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify for hosting

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our tools anonymously
  • Inspect our source code (it's open source)
  • Download and run the tools offline
  • Request deletion of any data (though we don't collect any)

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us.
